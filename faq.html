The basics
What is FundOff?
FundOff is a donation platform built on blockchain. The short version: GoFundMe can be ordered by a court to take down your campaign and freeze your funds. FundOff cannot. The funds go directly from donors to recipients through a smart contract that has no off switch, no admin keys, and no compliance team. We built it that way on purpose.
Why does this exist?
In June 2026 a judge issued a court order and GoFundMe removed a campaign that had raised $454,000 from thousands of donors. The family in need got nothing. The platform had no choice. We watched that happen and decided to build the platform where that is technically impossible. That is the whole origin story.

Trust and security
Why should I trust FundOff?
You should not trust us. Trust the math. Our smart contract is publicly readable at basescan.org. If the code says funds go to the recipient then funds go to the recipient. We cannot change that any more than we can change 2 plus 2. Every claim we make about how the platform works is verifiable by reading the contract code. We are not asking you to take our word for it.
Can FundOff steal my donations?
No. And not because we are nice people. Because we literally cannot. Funds go directly from donor to recipient through the smart contract. We never hold them. When we say we never touch your money we mean it in the most technical sense possible: the contract routes funds without our servers ever seeing them. This was an intentional design decision.
Verify it: 0x3a6a50a4c7145896BeBe99b731Ccf6Cb0739e752 on basescan.org. Read the donateUSDC function. The math does not lie.
Can FundOff be hacked?
The funds: Extremely unlikely. Funds are on Base blockchain. Attacking it would require attacking the Ethereum network which runs on hundreds of thousands of computers worldwide simultaneously. This has never happened.

The website: Any website can theoretically be attacked. We use Cloudflare for DDoS protection, Stripe for payment security, and Netlify for hosting. If the website went down the smart contract would keep running and funds already donated would be unaffected.
Is my payment information safe?
Your card details never touch our servers. Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified. That is the highest possible payment security certification. The same standard used by Amazon. We see a transaction confirmation. We never see your card number, CVV, or billing address.
Do you sell my data?
No. We do not sell data. We do not share data with advertisers. We collect the minimum we need to run the platform and nothing else. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
Campaigns
Do I need approval to start a campaign?
No. Campaigns go live the moment you create them. No review process. No waiting period. No human who decides if your cause is worthy. This is intentional. The right to ask people for help should not require a platform's permission.
Can FundOff take down my campaign?
From our website: yes. If a campaign violates our Terms of Service we can remove it from fundoff.org. We reserve this right and use it for campaigns involving violence, fraud, child exploitation, and similar serious violations.

From the blockchain: no. Removing a campaign from our website does not affect any funds already donated. Those belong to the recipient. A court order served to FundOff LLC cannot touch funds that are already on the blockchain.
What campaigns are not allowed?
Violence, terrorism, child exploitation, fraud, illegal weapons, and harassment. Full list in our Terms of Service. We are serious about these. We are not serious about much else. If your campaign is controversial but legal we are probably fine with it.
What is the minimum donation?
$20. This is enforced at the smart contract level. It cannot be worked around. The minimum exists to keep gas fees economically rational relative to donation size.
Is there a maximum donation?
No. Donate a million dollars if you want. We will not stop you. The smart contract does not care.
Receiving money
Do I need a crypto wallet to receive donations?
No. When creating a campaign you can choose to receive funds directly to your bank account via Stripe. Donations get converted to USD automatically and deposited daily. The blockchain is completely invisible to you. It feels like getting paid on Venmo.
How long does it take to receive funds?
Bank account: 1 to 2 business days after Stripe processes the payout. Crypto wallet: instant, the moment the transaction confirms on Base blockchain (usually under 30 seconds).
What are the fees?
Free tier: 2% of each donation. Pro ($9.99/month): 1%. Elite ($29.99/month): 0.5%. For comparison GoFundMe takes 2.9% plus $0.30 per transaction. On a $454,000 campaign that is a difference of over $5,000 in your pocket.
Can donations be refunded?
No. Blockchain transactions are final. This is not a policy decision. It is a technical fact about how blockchains work. Donors should be confident in a campaign before donating. We recommend checking the campaign's verification status and doing your own research.
What if
What if FundOff shuts down?
The smart contract keeps running. It is on Base blockchain which does not care whether our company exists. The code is open source so anyone can fork and redeploy the frontend. Funds already donated are safe regardless of what happens to us. We designed it this way deliberately.
What if a court orders FundOff to freeze funds?
We cannot comply even if we wanted to. Funds that have been donated are on the blockchain. We have no technical mechanism to freeze or reverse them. A court can order our website taken down. It cannot order Ethereum to stop processing transactions.
This is the same reason a court cannot order the internet to delete a webpage that exists on hundreds of servers worldwide. The architecture makes compliance impossible, not just unlikely.
What if I create a campaign and the goal is not reached?
You keep everything raised. FundOff is not Kickstarter. There is no all-or-nothing model. Every dollar donated goes to you regardless of whether you hit your goal.
What if I think a campaign is a scam?
Use the Report button on any campaign page or email reports@fundoff.org. We review reports within 24 hours. If a campaign is fraudulent we remove it from our website and, where possible, alert relevant authorities. We cannot recover funds already donated but we can stop new donations from being sent to bad actors.
