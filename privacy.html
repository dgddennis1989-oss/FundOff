Privacy Policy

FundOff is built on privacy by design. Because we use blockchain infrastructure, we collect far less data than traditional platforms. Here's exactly what we do and don't collect — and why.

📅 Last updated: June 2026
🏛️ FundOff DAO LLC · Wyoming, USA
📧 hello@fundoff.org

The short version: We don't sell your data. We don't store payment information. We don't require accounts. Most activity on FundOff happens directly on the blockchain — not through our servers. We collect only what's strictly necessary to run the platform.

01 Who We Are

FundOff is operated by FundOff DAO LLC, a Wyoming limited liability company. Our platform allows anyone to create and fund campaigns via blockchain smart contracts deployed on the Base network (Ethereum Layer 2).

Our smart contract address: 0x3a6a50a4c7145896BeBe99b731Ccf6Cb0739e752

02 What We Do NOT Collect

Unlike traditional donation platforms, FundOff does not:

  • Require you to create an account or provide personal information to donate
  • Store your payment card details — card processing is handled by third-party payment processors
  • Collect your name, address, or government ID for standard donations
  • Track you across other websites
  • Sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Store private keys or wallet credentials of any kind

03 What We Do Collect

Campaign Data
Title, description, goal amount, and wallet address submitted when creating a campaign. This is stored on-chain and is permanently public.
Donation Messages
Optional messages left by donors are stored on-chain and are permanently public. Do not include personal information in messages.
Report Submissions
If you submit a campaign report, we collect the information you provide in the report form including reason and description.
Contact Emails
If you email us directly we retain that correspondence to respond to your inquiry.
Basic Analytics
We may use privacy-friendly analytics (no cookies, no fingerprinting) to understand general traffic patterns such as page visits and geographic region.
Wallet Addresses
Wallet addresses used in transactions are recorded on the public blockchain. This is inherent to blockchain technology and outside our control.

04 The Blockchain Is Public

All transactions on FundOff are processed on the Base blockchain. This means:

  • All donations, campaign creations, and fee payments are permanently recorded on a public ledger
  • Wallet addresses involved in transactions are publicly visible
  • Transaction amounts and timestamps are publicly visible
  • This data cannot be deleted, modified, or made private — it is the nature of blockchain technology
  • FundOff does not control or have the ability to alter blockchain records

Important: Do not include personally identifiable information in campaign descriptions, donation messages, or wallet addresses. Once submitted on-chain this data is permanent and public.

05 Payment Processing

When you donate using a credit card, debit card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay, your payment is processed by third-party payment processors. FundOff does not see, store, or have access to your full card number, CVV, or banking credentials.

Our payment processors maintain their own privacy policies and security standards. We recommend reviewing their policies before making a payment.

For cryptocurrency donations made directly via wallet connection, no payment processor is involved. The transaction goes directly from your wallet to the smart contract.

06 Cookies

FundOff uses minimal cookies strictly necessary to operate the platform. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies that track you across websites.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use FundOff.

07 How We Use Information

Information we collect is used only for:

  • Operating and maintaining the FundOff platform
  • Reviewing campaign reports and enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Responding to support inquiries and emails
  • Understanding general usage patterns to improve the platform
  • Complying with legal obligations where required by law

08 Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With payment processors strictly to process your donation
  • With law enforcement if required by a valid legal order — however we note that blockchain transaction data is already publicly available and we have limited personal data to provide
  • To protect the rights or safety of FundOff, our users, or the public

09 Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Campaign reports — retained for 12 months or until resolved
  • Support emails — retained for 24 months
  • Analytics data — aggregated and anonymized, no individual retention
  • Blockchain data — permanent and outside our control

10 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have rights including:

  • The right to know what personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of personal data we hold (note: blockchain data cannot be deleted)
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal data
  • The right to object to processing of your personal data
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights contact us at hello@fundoff.org. We will respond within 30 days.

11 Children's Privacy

FundOff is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information please contact us immediately at hello@fundoff.org and we will take steps to delete it.

12 Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information we hold. These include:

  • HTTPS encryption on all web traffic
  • No storage of payment card data on our servers
  • Smart contract security — funds are never held by FundOff
  • Minimal data collection by design — we can't leak what we don't have

No security system is perfect. In the event of a data breach we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

13 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of FundOff after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes we will post a notice on the FundOff homepage.

14 Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Wyoming, United States. Any disputes relating to this policy shall be resolved in Wyoming courts.
